About Student Relationship Management


What is Student Relationship Management?

The University of Liverpool and the Department of Computer Science currently store a lot of information about students in multiple databases, which users access via many different systems. The use of too many systems poses time efficiency problems as well as a steep learning curve for any novice user, such as new students or lecturers. The proposed solution will help alleviate these issues and offer a central location for staff and students to access student information and communications.


How do I get help?

No matter whether you are a student, or staff of the University of liverpool, to get help you can press the 'Help (?)' button from an open navigation drawer. To open the navigation drawer, press the burger icon in the top left of the web page. The navigation drawer can be accessed from any web page.


How can I report a bug?

Bugs for Student Relationship Management can be reported by clicking the 'Report Bug' button from the Options section of the Navigation Drawer. For the developer to gain the best understanding of the bug, please be as detailed as possible when reporting.
